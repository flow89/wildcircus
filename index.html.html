Wild Circus

Welcome to the wild circus !

Travel with us and enter into a dreamlike and poetic universe mixing acrobatics to theatre in a subtle way.
At the begining, The Wild Circus Company was a street art company named "wild Company" and traveled all around Europe to convey a message of peace, happiness and good mood. They pursued with relentless passion a dream they believed and gained visibility throughout Europe and around the world. Then, the company changed her name to become "The wild circus" company, reflecting to their early years.
Come and share with us a magical and an unforgettable evening! Let the show begin !
